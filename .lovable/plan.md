

# Satsoko Hero Landing Page

## Overview
A minimalistic, catchy landing page for Satsoko — a Bitcoin buying service via M-Pesa (KES). Clean white background with bold orange accents matching the Satsoko brand.

## Layout & Structure

### 1. Navigation Bar
- Satsoko logo (Bitcoin-themed orange logo + "satsoko" text) on the left
- Navigation links on the right: **Buy Bitcoin**, **FAQ**, **Contact**
- Clean, minimal header with subtle bottom border

### 2. Hero Section
- **Main Headline**: Large, bold text — "Buy **bitcoin** instantly to your bitcoin wallet via Satsoko App"
  - The word "bitcoin" highlighted in orange (#E87B35) to pop against dark text
- **Subtitle**: Smaller text below — "Convert your KES to bitcoin instantly via mobile money"
- Centered layout with generous spacing

### 3. CTA Buttons (Below headline)
- Three buttons displayed inline:
  - **App Store** badge (black with Apple icon)
  - **Google Play** badge (black with Play icon)  
  - **Web App** button (orange, "Use Web App" with globe icon)
- Clean, spaced evenly in a row

### 4. Phone Mockup (Center)
- A CSS-built realistic iPhone mockup showing the Satsoko app interface:
  - Satsoko logo at top
  - "Buy bitcoin instantly via M-Pesa" heading
  - BTC price display (1 BTC ≈ USD rate in green)
  - Amount input field with KES currency selector and Kenyan flag
  - Satoshis conversion display
  - Lightning wallet address input
  - "Proceed to payment" orange button
  - WhatsApp and X social icons at the bottom
- Phone slightly tilted or with a subtle shadow for depth
- Floating against the white background with a soft glow/shadow

### 5. Background & Styling
- White clean background
- Subtle orange gradient or glow behind the phone mockup for visual interest
- Bitcoin-related faint watermark icons scattered in the background (optional, very subtle)
- Smooth, modern typography (Inter or system font)

## Design Details
- **Primary color**: Orange (#E87B35)
- **Text color**: Dark navy/charcoal for headlines
- **Accent**: Green for verified/success states in the mockup
- **Font sizes**: Large bold headline (4xl-6xl), medium subtitle, clean body text
- **Responsive**: Stacks vertically on mobile, headline shrinks gracefully
